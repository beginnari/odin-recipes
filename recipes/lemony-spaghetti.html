Pasta Al Limone

M. Amjahid, for Monopol magazine

Description

Quick and easy and delicious is how I want my food. This cheesy lemony spaghetti recipe is all that.

Many thanks to Mr. Mohamed Amjahid, a columnist I deeply appreciate. I do wish he’d add a list&steps summary ;)

Feeds two very hungry people. Or one Nari — is what I usually say, but not here! Seriously. Half, for one person.

Ingredients

  • lemon — one, zest and juice
  • spaghetti — 250g; wholegrain is nice if you have it
  • garlic — 4 cloves (listen to your heart)
  • hard cheese — grated, 100g pecorino, or parmesan, grana padano, whatevz
  • olive oil
  • salt, pepper — to taste
  • sage — basil, or other herbs - optional, add with the garlic if using)
  • Pasta Water! — half a cup, do NOT forget when draining pasta.

Steps

  1. Boil water in pot,add spaghetti, refer to package for how long
  2. Once spaghetti is in, put pan on medium heat (3/10), add olive oil and smashed/cut/minced garlic

    3. Fun Fact! Garlic will taste more intense depending on how it is cut. Thin slices will make it sweeter and less smelly. Smashed and cut: medium. Minced: more intense. Smashing and mincing destroys the cell walls, releasing the flavour, so cut if you want the taste to be more tender. (Or if you are a coward.) Or use less garlic! But why would you. Garlic!

  3. Zest and juice your lemon, grate cheese
  4. When pasta done, add lemon juice and zest to pan, let it sizzle. (Reserve a bit of zest for decoration)
  5. Add half a ladle of pasta water (less is more! easier ti add some than Wait for Food) and cheese, stir, add spaghetti, salt and pepper. Stir!
  6. Once suitably creamy, (this depends on how much pasta water you added) — decorate with a bit of zest and maybe a basil leaf, eat.

M. Amjahid, for Monopol magazine
